Cookies & Privacy — Quick Summary

We use cookies to ensure core functionality and (with consent) to collect anonymous analytics that help us improve class scheduling, equipment info, and site performance.

  • Essential: Sessions, security (always active).
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage metrics (enabled only with consent).
  • Retention: Consent stored for 30 days. Analytics aggregated and anonymized.

Privacy & Cookies — Q&A (Gym / Fitness)

1. What personal data do we collect?
Account info (name, email), membership and booking data, optional fitness profile (goals, preferences). Analytics data is anonymous.

2. Why cookies?
To maintain secure sessions, remember preferences, and anonymously measure site usage to improve student services.

3. Can I change consent?
Yes — open Manage Preferences on the banner at any time or clear cookies in your browser. Changes apply immediately on this page.

4. Do you share data?
Only with vetted service providers (hosting, payments, analytics). Analytics providers receive anonymized data; marketing vendors are used only with explicit opt-in.

5. How long do you keep data?
Consent: 30 days. Aggregated analytics: up to 12 months. Membership/account data: per university retention policy.
